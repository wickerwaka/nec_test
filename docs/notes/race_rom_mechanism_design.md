## Headlines
1. **The ROM is hiding a per-DIR-mode word race, not dense random logic.** Exact decomposition found: for each of the 4 (pre.DIR, pop.DIR) quadrants the class is a pure **staircase** `rank(pre6) >= thr(pop6)` over the remaining 6 flags {V,S,Z,AC,P,CY} — a total-order race between the two flag words — **plus only 68 exception cells of 16,384**, all of which sit in the ghost/resonance geography (below). The pre.DIR=0/pop.DIR=0 quadrant is a *perfect* staircase with just 3 distinct pre-levels and 4 pop-thresholds.
2. **The exceptions ARE the ghost's geography.** 32 of the 68 are ghost cells themselves (cells where the underlying race law says B but the shipped hex stores the bus-verified ghost-repair class A). The other 36 sit at pop words in/adjacent to the 8 ghost pop patterns ({DIR,S,V,Z}±CY, {CY,P}±V±DIR, {Z}±V±DIR), paired with "resonant" pre words ({S,Z}-core or {CY,P/DIR}-core). One anomaly, two observers: the A/B class flip and the pending-latch clear failure share a localized disturbance zone; its core words are **PLA-contradictory flag combos** (Z=1 with S=1 or P=0 — impossible as a real ALU result) and words with 1s in PSW bits 11/10 (V,DIR), the two physical neighbors of the IE bit (9) that the dispatch CITF force-clears.
3. **Bit-exact compact replacement built and verified**: 6 small tables (two 64×6 pre-rank tables selected by pre.DIR, four 64×7 pop-threshold tables selected by quadrant) + a `pre7==pop7 → A` diagonal guard + 68 enumerated exception cells = **16384/16384 exact vs int9d_race.hex** (prototype race_rom_race_law.svh, generator race_rom_emit_sv.py, both in scratchpad). ~2.3 kbit of case-table logic is PREDICTED to replace the 16-kbit M9K ROM and retire the `$readmemh`/SEARCH_PATH idiom — resource/timing/acceptance claims are predictions until the R0b Quartus spike reports (Codex F2).
4. Negative results (exact, worth recording): the function is **NOT** any `g(pre7) > h(pop7)` two-race (48% of row pairs incomparable — the DIR bits interact with the opponent word), NOT a sum-of-transition-costs threshold (best exact fit 2620 mismatches), NOT quadratic (best 1511), NOT a monotone scalar accumulator in any flag order (chain test fails at every layer). The failed-algebra note in interrupt_model.md ("ANF ~2000 terms, degree 13, asymmetric") is exactly the signature of a staircase family glued across 4 modes with a localized overlay.

## Groundwork verified (existing data first)
- Table = 16,384 cells, classes B 8568 / A 7464 / AB 128 / ghost-OTHER 224. AB cells are exactly the pre7==pop7 diagonal (128 = all of it); hex stores A there and on all ghosts; hex matches JSON on every A/B cell (0 diffs).
- Influence (fraction of determinate flip-pairs changing class): pre.DIR .559, pre.S .360, pre.Z .277, pre.P .276, pre.AC .242 >> pop.S .160, pop.AC .158, pop.P .130, pop.Z .126 >> pop.CY .040, pop.DIR .030, pop.V .030, pre.CY .005, pre.V .002. pre.V/CY dependence = only ~60 flip-pairs, ALL at ghost-family pop patterns.
- µcode ground truth (docs/V20UC.TXT via V20UCDIS.PAS): POP PSW 9D = µ0078-007B with `007A OPR->FLAGS  F E`; PUSH PSW 9C `0075 FLAGS->OPR` (E, **no F**); SAHF 9E `007E tmpa->FLAGS` (no F); IRET `01EA OPR->FLAGS F E`; INT dispatch `01F4 FLAGS->OPR F`, `01F5 ... CTL CITF MEMW SS` (push of the popped image + IE/TF clear), `01FC SIGMA->SP E CTL FLUSH`. Statistically F tags OPR-coupled transfers (65/68 F-moves have OPR as source; the F-tagged x->OPR loads are exactly the ones decoupled from a later MEMW µline: INT/CALLF/PUSHA/ENTER) — a bus-side OPR handshake, consistent with FLAGS→bus traffic passing through a shared drive path at dispatch.

## The law (engineering statement — this is what ships)
Let pre7/pop7 = {V,DIR,S,Z,AC,P,CY} of psw_old/psw (the existing r9d_pre/r9d_pop wires, v30_eu.sv:598-601). Let d=pre7[5](=pre.DIR), q=pop7[5]; rp/rq = the 6-bit words {V,S,Z,AC,P,CY}.

```
race_B = (pre7 == pop7) ? A                          // 128 AB cells (hex holds A)
       : ( G_d[rp] >= H_{d,q}[rq] ) XOR EXC(pre7,pop7)
```
- G_0, G_1: 64-entry 6-bit rank tables (one per pre.DIR value; the pre-word order is shared by both pop.DIR quadrants of that d — verified: foreign-order transfer costs 0-84 within same d, 456-934 across d).
- H_00..H_11: 64-entry 7-bit threshold tables (values 0..64). Distinct threshold levels per quadrant: 4 / 7 / 4 / 6 — the staircases are COARSE; the 64-entry tables carry mostly-redundant structure that synthesis will collapse.
- EXC: 68 fixed 14-bit addresses (32 ghost-repair + 36 resonance cells). Frozen list emitted by the generator.
- Exact-match ledger of every family tried (16,256 determinate cells): raw-linear 2836; transition-cost sum 2620; +all-pairs quadratic 1511; single rank + quadrant offsets 360; per-side DIR ranks 304; independent-order 4-staircase 56; shared-order 4-staircase 68; **staircase+EXC+diagonal 0**. Layered minimal automaton (order AC,Z,S,P,CY,DIR,V; widths 1,4,15,22,19,12,7,2; 82 states) = exact by construction, kept as fallback implementation only.
- Caveat, stated honestly: G/H/EXC are FITTED (hill-climbed; 68 is an upper bound, not proven minimal — independent per-quadrant orders reach 56). The *decomposition shape* (per-quadrant staircase + resonance overlay) is the discovery; the specific tables are a fingerprint. The implementation copies the hex bit-for-bit regardless, so no measured behavior rides on the fit being "true".

## Mechanism account (science — separate from the predicate)
**DERIVED (exact, from the table):**
- The race quantity is a property of the two flag WORDS as wholes, per DIR mode — not a per-bit independent race (two-race family excluded exactly), not additive costs (sum families fail), not any monotone accumulator (chain/Dilworth tests fail: min chain-cover 11).
- pre.DIR selects between two genuinely different pre-word orders (rank correlation across d: −0.18) — DIR is a MODE, not a delay term.
- FITTED-EXACT (robust across fits), relabeled per Codex F7: the anomaly overlay (exceptions + ghost) is localized at specific pop words — the {DIR,S,V,Z}±CY core (result-inconsistent: S&Z, Z&!P), the {CY,P}±V±DIR core, and {Z}-alone. Robustness run (2026-07-23): the independent-per-quadrant zero-error fit's 56 exceptions are a strict SUBSET of the shared-order fit's 68; both draw from the same 6 pop families and both contain exactly the 32 ghost-repair cells — the localization persists across both known zero-error decompositions, but other decompositions are conceivable, hence FITTED-EXACT not DERIVED. Genuinely DERIVED (raw-table, fit-free): 162/224 ghost cells lie in the 2048-cell pop{V=1,DIR=1,AC=0} region (ghost locations are measured, not fitted), and the pre.V/pre.CY-sensitive flip-pairs cluster at the same pop families (raw influence analysis, pre-staircase).
**Constrained by measurement (from interrupt_model.md):** the same table governs one-NOP-late races → the pre image physically survives ≥1 instruction past POP PSW retirement; pushed PSW = popped image in both classes → the dispatch's FLAGS read (µ01F4) sees the popped image; pre-IE=0 never races.
**SPECULATIVE (physical narrative, clearly labeled):** consistent story — flags live in two physical stores: the µcode-bus-addressable FLAGS row (gets the popped image at µ007A, sourced by µ01F4's push read) and the live/EU-side latch row (holds the pre image; conditional-jump/PLA side). A refresh of the live row from the bus row is triggered lazily and races the dispatch's CITF strobe (µ01F5), which force-clears IE(9)/TF(8) in the live row: refresh completes first → live=popped (A); CITF first → refresh aborted, live keeps pre (B), IE/TF cleared either way. The race outcome depends on both words through drive-fight resolution per DIR mode; words with 1s on the IE/TF neighbors (V=11, DIR=10) and PLA-contradictory combos disturb the strobe enough to also leave the INT-pending latch set (ghost). This story fits every constraint but is NOT testable from the existing corpus alone — it is a narrative, not a law; do not encode it anywhere.
